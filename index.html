The Reality of Adoption

Analysis of your pre-session feedback reveals a fascinating divide in how AI impacts our daily workflows.

The Efficiency Paradox

Colleagues using AI daily report significant increases in operational efficiency.

Conversely, occasional users report decreased efficiency. This is the learning curve—treating AI like Google search yields poor results and costs more time than it saves.

The Confidence Gap

High confidence is directly correlated with using AI for targeted, specific tasks (like resource adaptation) rather than open-ended tasks.

Anxiety surrounding ethics and accuracy is highest among those who have not yet established a formal framework for prompting.

Governance & Ethics

Administrative Procedure 142

Establishing parameters for safe, ethical, and effective utilization of AI in EIPS.

1. Data Privacy

Administrative Procedure 142 prohibits the input of Personally Identifiable Information (PII) into public AI models. Pseudonyms and general descriptors are required to sanitize data.

2. Accuracy & Accountability

AI models can hallucinate. Professionals remain accountable for the output and are required to verify it for accuracy, reliability, and lack of bias.

3. Cognitive Offloading

AI is designed to augment professional judgment. It does not replace pedagogical decision-making or the evaluation of student learning.

Connecting Policy to Practice

Answering Your Survey Questions

"What information should you or should you not share when interacting with AI?"

Answer: AP 142 Section 2

Strict prohibition of any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) regarding staff or students.

"What are the optics of using AI? It feels like cheating on some levels."

Answer: Academic Integrity Framework

Using an intern to draft is not cheating if the professional rigorously reviews, edits, and takes final ownership of the output.

"Are there any AI tools that will not store or share any information that I put in?"

Answer: Yes. Enterprise-Protected Tools.

When logged in with EIPS credentials, specific approved tools guarantee your data is isolated and never used to train base models.

Lesson Design & Co-Creation

Make a lesson plan about Ancient Athens for Grade 6.
Write a 70-minute lesson plan for Grade 6 Social Studies about Athenian direct democracy. Include an engaging activity.
[Prepare] I am an Alberta Grade 6 Social Studies teacher. I need a lesson aligned with the provincial curriculum focusing on democratic principles. [Prime] Resources to incorporate: excerpts on Athenian direct democracy (citizens/metics/slaves, lottery, debate, secret ballots), and Canadian parliamentary structure for comparison. [Partner] Design a 70-min, student-centred lesson that: 1) launches with guiding question 'How can history provide insight into modern democracies?'; 2) uses jigsaw groups; 3) culminates in a Socratic seminar creating a Venn diagram of ancient vs. Canadian principles; 4) embeds a competency-based rubric. [Polish] Provide equity checks (gender/exclusion in ancient Athens), Indigenous perspectives, adaptations (visual supports), and a 3-question exit reflection. Output in table form.

Understanding Multimodality

AI is no longer just a text-based chatbot. It can process and generate across multiple modes of communication. Click to flip.

Vision

(Image-to-Text)

Vision

AI can "see". You can upload a photo of a handwritten math problem or a complex diagram, and the AI can transcribe it, explain it step-by-step, or translate it into another language.

Image Generation

(Text-to-Image)

Image Generation

AI can create original visuals from text descriptions. This is invaluable for creating highly specific visual supports, social stories, or presentation graphics without endless searching.

Audio & Voice

(Text-to-Audio / Audio-to-Text)

Audio & Voice

AI can generate natural, conversational audio from text, or transcribe spoken audio into structured notes. This bridges the gap for auditory learners and supports executive functioning.

Research & Synthesis

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Solving Hallucinations with NotebookLM

Unlike standard chat models which search the entire open web, RAG tools only read the documents you explicitly upload. By restricting the AI's knowledge base to your trusted sources, RAG effectively eliminates hallucination.

System Automation

What is "Vibe Coding and Agents"?

The practice of interacting with AI using natural language to generate, debug, and deploy computer code without needing to know traditional programming syntax.


You provide the "vibe" (the human intent, logic, and workflow).
The AI acts as the translator, writing the script to execute it.


Old Paradigm

Learn to Code ➔ Open Complex Software ➔ Type Code by Hand ➔ Fix Breaking Errors ➔ Deploy

New Paradigm

Describe What You Want ➔ AI Translates to Code ➔ AI Fixes Any Bugs ➔ Deploy

Generating the Script
